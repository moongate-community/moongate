[Title] Your New Aquarium
[Author] Les Bilgewater
[ReadOnly] True

Welcome to the
wonderful world
of aquarium ownership.
With a little time and
skill your aquarium can
become a work of art!
Catching Fish:
You will need to
aquire a fishing net,
and a number of fish
bowls, from your local
fisherman.
To use the net,
select it from your
backpack, and cast it
into shallow water.
Then we wait.
  The fish will be so
happy to be in your
aquarium, they will
jump right into your
fish bowl when caught!
Just take them home
and pour them into
your aquarium, and
BING! You have a happy
new addition to your
collection!
Caring for our
Underwater Allies:

  Fish need clean
water and food to
survive.
  Our aquarium comes
with a status monitoring
aid.  Must be Magic!
  Just look at the
front of your aquarium
(mouse over). See the
helpful and friendly
guide? It tells how much
food and water is
needed to maintain,
and improve the quality
of your tank.
  You dont want to
overfeed your fish
very often.  In fact,
you only want to feed
them every other day.
But, remember to keep
the water strong and
healthy, and add more
on a regular basis
to keep your aquarium
a pretty, sparkely blue.

  Well, I hope you
get as much enjoyment,
collecting a beautiful
array of our fine, finned,
friends from the sea,
as I do!

Happy Fishing!
